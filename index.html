We the Team Shaashwat

And We are

Exploring new things!

Shaashwat is an innovative ecosustainability club at RSCOE, spearheaded by Mr. Ashish Joshi, a former civil engineering student of the institution. Founded with a vision to promote ecological consciousness, Shaashwat strives to instill sustainable practices within the college community.

Vision

To inspire college youth to reach their fullest potential in engineering by providing them with access to exciting authentic, hands on activity, enthusiastic engineering mentors, and a supportive environment to encourage them to come out with innovative  ideas.

Mission

Help in the betterment of the campus of JSPM'S RSCOE revamp of the paper and plastic recycling the paper and plastic recycling programs. Participation in the Environment sustanable activities, river clean ups, arrangement of expert lectures for in-depth technical knowledge and continue to educate our community about conservation and healthy living.
